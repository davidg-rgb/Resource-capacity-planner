<!-- ADR-010 — Largest-remainder algorithm -->
1. quota = floor(N*100 / K) / 100  (work in cents to avoid float)
2. remainder_cents = round(N*100) - quota_cents * K
3. First `remainder_cents` slots get +0.01; rest get the base quota.
4. Pure function, deterministic, exact-sum at numeric(5,2) precision.
